ANDROID SMARTPHONE
1. Tap on "WLAN" and select Scenic Guest. 
2. Enter the password Spaceship1 and click on the "View more" option.
3. Scroll down to MAC address type and select the "Phone MAC" option.
4. You are then connected to the WLAN. 

APPLE IOS SMARTPHONE
1. Select the Scenic Guest WLAN and enter the password Spaceship1.
2. Click on the blue "i" symbol.
3. Deactivate the private WLAN address and click on "Next".
4. Exit the "Settings" and then you are connected to the WLAN.
